FAQs

What is lyntra?

Lyntra is an AI-powered web design tool that helps users create custom portfolios and websites in minutes. By simply entering a few key details such as your style preference and resume, you can receive a fully personalized, professional website without the need for coding or maintenance. Lyntra is built to streamline the web design process, making it accessible for anyone looking to showcase their skills, experience, and personal brand online.

Who is lyntra for?

Did you know that 71% of employers agree that having a portfolio website influences their hiring decisions, but less than 20% of people actually have a personal portfolio?

Lyntra is designed for students, job seekers, and professionals looking to build a personal portfolio. It’s ideal for those who want to create a standout online presence quickly and easily. Whether you're just starting your career or looking to advance, Lyntra allows you to present your work in a polished, professional way without the need for design or coding skills. It's also perfect for entrepreneurs and professionals who need a fast, simple solution to get a custom website up and running.
